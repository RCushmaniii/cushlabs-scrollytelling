# Ambient Bed Prompts — ElevenLabs SFX API

All ambient beds: 30 seconds, looping enabled, 48kHz WAV.
Volume in mix: 0.15-0.25 (subtle background layer).

---

## 01 — Hero Ambient
```
Deep cinematic drone, warm and inviting. Subtle low-frequency hum with gentle
harmonic overtones. Feels like the opening shot of a film. Slow evolving pad
with barely perceptible digital shimmer. Dark, elegant, premium. No rhythm,
no melody — pure atmosphere.
```

## 02 — Problem Ambient
```
Tense atmospheric drone with subtle dissonance. Uneasy undertone suggesting
something isn't right. Faint digital static and glitch textures woven into a
dark pad. Slightly claustrophobic. Builds subtle unease without being
aggressive. Corporate tension. Cold digital hum.
```

## 03 — Solution Ambient
```
Confident electronic ambient pulse. Warm golden tone with subtle rhythmic
energy building underneath. Digital textures that feel purposeful and precise.
Evolving synth pad with faint ascending harmonics suggesting breakthrough and
clarity. Optimistic but grounded. Tech-forward cinematic atmosphere.
```

## 04 — Proof Ambient
```
Warm, resolved ambient pad. Gentle and open. Feels like arrival — the tension
has released. Subtle organic warmth mixed with soft digital textures. Faint
string-like harmonics in the background. Trustworthy, accomplished, calm
confidence. Documentary film score atmosphere.
```

## 05 — Ask Ambient
```
Open, inviting ambient pad. Minimal and spacious. Warm low-end with gentle
high-frequency shimmer. Feels like an open door — welcoming, unhurried.
Slowly breathing texture that fades toward silence. Intimate and personal.
Final scene of a film before credits.
```
